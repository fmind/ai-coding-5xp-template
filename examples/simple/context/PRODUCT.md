# Product Context

**Objective**: A personal portfolio website to showcase my freelance work, blog posts, and connect with potential clients.

## Objective & Vision

- **Elevator pitch**: A clean, lightning-fast portfolio site demonstrating my capabilities and past projects.
- **Long-term vision**: Serve as my primary inbound marketing channel for freelance gigs for the next decade.
- **Value proposition**: Centralized, professional hub that establishes authority and removes friction for hiring.

## Target Audience

- **Usage context**: Mostly desktop (during work hours) or mobile (when sharing links directly)
- **Primary users**: Tech recruiters, freelance clients looking for reliable independent developers
- **Secondary users**: Fellow developers interested in learning from my blog posts

## Features & Scope

- **Key features**: Project gallery, about me section, contact information, basic blog listing
- **Out of scope**: Interactive forms requiring a backend, user accounts, comments section
- **Future roadmap**: Adding RSS feed for blog capabilities

## Business Logic & Rules

- **Core domain rules**: The site serves as a funnel to redirect users to email me directly.
- **Edge cases**: Ensuring images load performantly on slow networks.
- **Compliance / Privacy**: No cookies are used, so no banner is required.

## Success Criteria & Constraints

- **Usage limits**: N/A (Static site)
- **Success metrics**: Homepage loads in under 1 second on mobile networks.
- **Absolute constraints**: Cost must remain essentially zero; relying entirely on free tier static hosting.
